Bitumen

Bitumen or asphalt, used interchangeably, is a black semi-solid hydrocarbon product produced by refining crude oil but can also occur naturally. It is very heavy, viscous characteristics, and is highly resistant to water as well as most environmental factors.

Aureum Myanmar sources the highest quality palm oil or vegetable cooking oil from the largest producing countries in the world such as Malaysia and Indonesia.
