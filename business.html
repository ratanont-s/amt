Business with Aureum
Myanmar Group of Companies

When dealing with any client, we prioritize our focus into client satisfaction before, during, and after the sale has been made. Our purpose is not to make short-term deals and businesses but to accumulate long-term partnerships and guarantee their satisfaction. We believe this is the way to achieve sustainable prosperity for both the supplier and the client.

In order to do so, we have invested heavily in our supply chain, distribution channels and quality assurances. In each shipment, we use SGS as the international independent surveyor at both the loading port as well as the discharging port. SGS is one of the most reputable if not the most trusted world renowned third-party surveyor to ensure quality and quantity for our customers.

Aureum Myanmar Group of Companies is able to support long-term infrastructure projects. With logistic systems in the Northern region and Southern region, we can steadily supply contract-term projects for a period of many months or years. The most important key factors for infrastructure contractors are stable supply and pricing during their projects. We invite contractors to meet and discuss the project terms with us to secure steady Bitumen supply and fixed pricing for the duration of the project.
