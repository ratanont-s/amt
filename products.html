Paraffin Wax

Paraffin Wax is a petroleum-based wax which is produced in the lubricant as well as base oil refining process. It is an organic, plastic–like substance that is solid at ambient temperature and becomes liquid when melted. Although its uses may have some cosmetic and therapeutic benefits, the majority of its uses are in the candle-manufacturing industry. Paraffin candles are odorless and bluish-white in colour, burns more cleanly and are more cost-effective than other types of candles.

With over 20 years of experience in this field, we have achieved and maintained our position as the top distributor supplying to the majority of the demand in Myanmar in terms of volume; several thousand tons per month.
